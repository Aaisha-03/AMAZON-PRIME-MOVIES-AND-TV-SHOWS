# AMAZON-PRIME-MOVIES-AND-TV-SHOWS
<img width="1156" height="646" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b540f3b7-1bc2-4a30-86d5-35d3fe6b0ef1" />

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing the Amazon Prime Video dataset.
The dashboard provides valuable insights into Prime Video’s movies and TV shows, including ratings, genres, directors, release years, and more — giving a complete view of the platform’s content distribution.
Key Insights:
Total Titles: 9,655
Total Ratings: 25
Total Genres: 519
Total Directors: 5,771
Data Range: 1920 – 2021
Main Visuals:
Rating by Total Shows: Shows distribution by audience rating (13+, 16+, R, etc.)
Genres by Total Shows: Popular genres such as Drama, Comedy, and Documentary.
Total Shows by Country: World map view showing show distribution by country.
Movie vs TV Shows: Pie chart representing the proportion of Movies (80.82%) vs TV Shows (19.18%).
Total Shows by Release Year: Trend of content released over time.
